Reisch takes over as commander of SD National Guard
RAPID CITY, S.D. - Leadership changes in the South Dakota National Guard will be made Saturday. Brig. Gen. Timothy Reisch (rysch) will be installed as state adjutant general in charge of the Army National Guard and Air National Guard. He replaces the retiring Maj. Gen. Steven Doohen in the top spot. Col. Jeffrey Marlette will replace Reisch as assistant state adjutant general. Both change-of-command ceremonies will be in Rapid City.
