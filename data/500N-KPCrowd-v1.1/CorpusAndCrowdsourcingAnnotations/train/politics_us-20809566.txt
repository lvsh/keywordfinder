Top evasion from the briefing
In Wednesday’s White House briefing, press secretary Jay Carney wouldn’t say whether President Obama would veto budget proposals that include riders. “We do not believe that this is the proper place to insert an ideologically driven agenda” into the budget, Carney said, calling the appropriations process a “byzantine and complex one.” He added, though, that “a rider can be something that declares apple pie the most favorite dessert in America.”
